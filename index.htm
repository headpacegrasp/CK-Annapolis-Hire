Your Onboarding Journey

We set you up for success from day one with structured training and mentorship.

1

Apply & Interview

In-person or video interview with our team. We want to know your story and what drives you.

2

Screening & Onboarding

Background check, TB screening, reference verification, and Viventium HR onboarding all coordinated for you.

3

CareAcademy Training

Online modules covering client rights, infection control, dementia care, fall prevention, emergency procedures, and more.

4

Hands-On Skills Validation

In-person skills check with a supervisor: transfers, personal care, hand hygiene, Viv app training, and Interactive Caregiving techniques.

5

Your First Client Match

We carefully match you based on skills, schedule, personality, and preferences then support you with 30/60/90-day check-ins.
