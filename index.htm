Your Onboarding Journey

We set you up for success from day one with paid, structured training and mentorship.

1

Apply & Interview

In-person or video interview with our team. We want to know your story and what drives you to caregiving.

2

Screening & Onboarding

Background check, drug screening, TB verification, reference checks, and Viventium HR onboarding — all coordinated for you.

3

Paid Orientation & CareAcademy Training

Online modules covering client rights, infection control, dementia care, fall prevention, emergency procedures, and Interactive Caregiving™ - all on the clock.

4

Hands-On Skills Validation

In-person skills check with a supervisor: transfers, personal care, hand hygiene, Viv app training, and Dementia Specialist Certification prep.

5

Your First Client Match

We carefully match you based on skills, schedule, personality, and preferences, then support you with 30/60/90-day check-ins and ongoing education.

🏅

Earn Your Dementia Specialist Certification

Through our training program, you'll earn a Dementia Specialist Certification that's recognized industry-wide and good for any employer for 2 years. It's a career credential you take with you our investment in your professional growth.

Portable Credential • 2-Year Validity
